One of the points that we think is critical for people to understand is that these are not unrelated fields.

Viruses and cancer-potentiating exosomes are subcellular particles, share a common set of biophysical characteristics, mainly they are approximately the same size and both particles are enclosed by membranes that are heavily covered with a specific sugar called mannose. The leptin protein GNA, which is contained in the Hemopurifier, specifically binds two mannose molecules allowing the cartridge to clear both types of particles.
