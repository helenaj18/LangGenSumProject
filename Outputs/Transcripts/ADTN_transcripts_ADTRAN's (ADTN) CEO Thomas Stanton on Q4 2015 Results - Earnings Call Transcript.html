End-to-end fiber connectivity is the ultimate goal for most carriers however due to cost network architecture and regulatory constraints, copper connectivity remains a reality.

Technology such bonding, vectoring, super vectoring and G.fast allow carriers to deliver over 1 gig utilizing their existing infrastructure.
