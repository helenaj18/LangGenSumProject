In some patients DSUVIA is used as the initial opioid dosed in the recovery room when there is concern that IV opioid bolus administration might be too much for the patient to tolerate. DSUVIA has also been helpful for our patients who have passed through the initial phase of recovery, are getting ready to be discharged have their IV removed and then all of a sudden have a flare of pain. In this situation, our experience is that oral opioids often did not provide time relief. Time in the recovery room is expensive and the number one issue for delayed discharges pain.
