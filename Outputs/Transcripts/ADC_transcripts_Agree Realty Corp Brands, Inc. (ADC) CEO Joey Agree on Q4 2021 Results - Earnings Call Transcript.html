As a result of our capital markets activities, our balance sheet is exceptionally well-positioned to start the year and affords us tremendous flexibility.

The aggregate dilutive impact related to these offerings was less than $0.005 to both core FFO and AFFO per share for the fourth quarter and approximately $0.01 for the 12 month period. Our consistent and reliable earnings growth continues to support a growing and well-covered dividend.

For the full year, the company declared dividends of just over $2.60 per share and 8.3% year-over-year increase and a 14% increase on a two-year stack basis.
