 our underlying combined ratio of 99.4% improved 8.8% year-over-year, which included 16 points of current year catastrophe losses, totaling $29.8 million. 
 a modest favorable reserve development of $823,000 led to an underlying combined ratio of 83.7%, which compares very favorably to 91.8% last year. 
 we had a heavy non-named cat quarter in the second quarter and a named storm with modest net loss. 
 we delivered a core income of $0.20 per share per share excluding named storms and a net loss of 54.8%, which improved over 6 points compared to the second quarter last year. 
 we continue to improve our non-cat combined ratio with an underlying combined ratio of 29.5% and a net loss ratio of 54.8%.
