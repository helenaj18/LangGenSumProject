 the second quarter of 2021 was a strong quarter for the Janice s company. 
 total revenues increased 8.5% year-over-year and our second full quarter as a standalone public company, primarily due to strong underlying business trends and the execution of our strategic priorities. 
 same store revenues grew 11.2% in the second quarter of 2021, compared to the prior year, the fifth sequential quarter of positive growth and the eighth positive quarter of the last 10 quarters. Adjusted EBITDA was $65.3 million for the second quarter of 2021 compared with $56.2 million for the same period in 2020, an increase of $9.1 million or 16.3%. 
 adjusted EBITDA margin improved 100 basis points from the prior year second quarter, and the fifth straight quarter of year-over-year adjusted EBITDA margin expansion. ecommerce revenues increased 15.8% versus the second quarter of 2020 and represented 14% of overall lease revenues compared to 12.8% in the second quarter of the prior year. our overall lease portfolio continues to perform well.  we ended the second quarter of 2021 with a lease portfolio size for all company operated stores of $132.8 million, an increase of 7.6% compared to a lease portfolio size of $123.4 million on June 30 of last year.  the favorable impact of centralized decisioning and continued but moderating government stimulus to our customers led to an 8.7% increase in our quarter ending same store lease portfolio size compared to the prior year. at the same time, the favorable impact of centralized lease positioning, our digital servicing platforms and other operational enhancements are contributing to a sustainable improvement in customer payment and write-off activity, resulting in fewer lease merchandise write-offs in both our store and ecommerce channels.
