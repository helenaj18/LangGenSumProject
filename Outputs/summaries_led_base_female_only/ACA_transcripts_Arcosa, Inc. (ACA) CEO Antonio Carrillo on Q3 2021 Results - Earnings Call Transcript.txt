 we report record third quarter revenue and adjusted EBITDA of $272 million and adjusted EBITDA of $280 million, respectively.  
 these results were driven by double-digit growth in both our Construction Products and Engineered Structures segments, more than offsetting lower year-over-year results in Transportation Products.  
 our third quarter adjusted EBITDA increased at a compounded annual growth rate of 21% while we have improved our margin by 240 basis points or nearly 20% over the last three years through the evolution of our portfolio towards faster growth higher margin businesses and by improving operational efficiencies.    
 we also expanded our third quarter adjusted EBITda in our wind towers business by 60% to approximately $175 million.    
 we continue to see favorable market drivers in our Construction and Engineered Structures businesses supporting our outlook for growth in the fourth quarter and into 2022.    
 finally, we also discuss the integration of the recent acquisitions of StonePoint and Southwest Rock.
