 we report on our financial results for the second quarter of 2017 inclusive of the addition of our new joint catastrophe reinsurance program with American Coastal. for the quarter we grew our policy count net 4.8% almost a 20% annualized organic growth rate, which is excellent on a book of almost 500,000 policies in force. net premiums earned of $160 million, up 40% from the same period a year ago. Operating income increased year-over-year for the quarter, but declined approximately 19% on a diluted per share basis. GAAP net income of $7.3 million or earnings per share of $0.17 included cat loss of approximately $22 million or $0.34 a share after tax. for the year operating earnings per share was $0.77 versus $0.73 up 6% from the same period a year ago. the underlying combined ratio was 83.7% and our book value per share increase to $12.39 per share. our non-cat loss ratio is down dramatically year-over-year mostly due to the inclusion of amCo this year, but our personal lines non-cat loss ratio has also shown steady improvement. on the commercial line side, we are maintaining our policy count and retaining business at almost 90% rate. Premium pressure seems to a stabilized and there are some very positive trends in this business. finally expenses, once you reverse the distorted effect of the quarter share treaty and back out non-recurring merger costs our expense ratio is down significantly year-over-year.
