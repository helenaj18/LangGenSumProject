 the Lori's Company, Inc. (NYSE:AAN ) second quarter of 2021 earnings were $1.05 per diluted share, an increase of 26.5% over the same quarter in the prior year. on a same store basis, revenues increased 11.2% in the second quarter of 2021, the fifth sequential quarter of positive growth and the eighth positive quarter of the last 10 quarters. Adjusted EBITDA was $65.3 million for the second quarter of 2021 compared with $56.2 million for the same period in 2020, an increase of $9.1 million or 16.3% and the fifth straight quarter of year-over-year adjusted EBITDA margin expansion.    
 this is the second full quarter as a standalone public company and our second full quarter as a result of the spin off of the company.    
 we continue to execute on our multiyear strategy of promoting the Lori s value proposition, digitizing the customer experience and aligning our store footprint to the customer opportunity. in the second quarter of 2021, we opened 64 new stores and we expect to open more than 60 new stores in the second half of 2021.    
 we also continue to invest in our real estate strategy that is accelerating with the growing store pipeline and performance that is exceeding our expectations.    
 our ecommerce revenues increased 15.8% in the second quarter of 2021 and represented 14% of overall lease revenues compared to 12.8% in the second quarter of the prior year.    
 we continue to see strong demand for our products and continued but moderating government stimulus to our customers led to an 8.7% increase in our quarter ending same store lease portfolio size compared to the prior year.    
 our overall lease portfolio continues to perform well.    
 we expect to continue to operate in a 5% to 5% annual write-off percentage
