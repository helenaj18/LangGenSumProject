 we report first quarter net sales of $1.3 billion and comparable store sales of negative 1% to flat. 
 gross profit margin was 43% compared with 44.6% in the prior year. on a rate basis, gross margin declined 2.6% compared with 6% in the previous year. 
 we also incurred a headwind of $17 million in SG&A associated with the prior year adjustments. 
 the combination of gross margin and SG&A deleverage resulted in an operating margin decline of 339 basis points in the quarter. 
 we also reduced our quarterly cash dividend to $0.25 this quarter. 
 we are updating our full year guidance to include net sales of $11.2 billion to $11.3 billion, comparable store sales of negative 1% to flat, GAAP operating income margin of 5% to 5.3, income tax rate of 24% to 25, diluted earnings per share of $6 to $6.50, capital expenditures of $250 million to $300 million, a range of $200 million to $300 million in free cash flow and 40 to 60 new store and branch openings.
