 the Helen's Company, Inc. (NYSE:AAN ) second quarter of 2021 earnings were $1.05 per diluted share, an increase of 26.5% over the same quarter in the prior year. on a same store basis, revenues increased 11.2% in the second quarter of 2021, the fifth sequential quarter of positive growth and the eighth positive quarter of the last 10 quarters. Adjusted EBITDA was $65.3 million for the second quarter of 2021 compared with $56.2 million for the same period in 2020, an increase of $9.1 million or 16.3% and the fifth straight quarter of year-over-year adjusted EBITDA margin expansion.    
 this is the second full quarter as a standalone public company and our second full quarter as a result of the spin off.    
 we continue to see strong demand for our products and continued but moderating government stimulus to our customers.    
 our ecommerce revenues increased 15.8% versus the second quarter of 2020 and represented 14% of overall lease revenues compared to 12.8% in the second quarter of the prior year.    
 we ended the second quarter of 2021 with a lease portfolio size for all company operated stores of $132.8 million, an increase of 7.6% compared to a lease portfolio size of $123.4 million on June 30 of last year.    
 the improvement in write- offs was primarily due to the continued favorable impact of our in-store and online decision technologies, strong operational execution and the benefit to our customers from government stimulus.    
 our overall lease portfolio continues to perform well.   
 we ended the second quarter with a quarter ending same store lease portfolio size of 8.7%, an increase of 8.7% compared to the prior year.
