On August 10, 2020, The Equity Group announced the resumption of its overseas programs.  According to the SEC's complaint filed in the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York, ATA Creativity Global (AACG) and its CFO, Ruobai Sima, engaged in a series of unannounced phone calls with students in China and elsewhere.  The SEC's complaint alleges that, in the first half of the year, students in China and elsewhere began to apply for courses in the company's portfolio of services, which included portfolio training and overseas study consulting services.  The complaint further alleges that, in the second half of the year, more than half of those students were Chinese.  The complaint further alleges that, despite limited revenue contributions from the company's portfolio training services business, more than half of those students were actually Chinese.
