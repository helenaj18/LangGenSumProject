On October 26, 2022, the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") announced that American Assets Trust, Inc. ("AAT"), a San Diego-based real estate investment trust, announced third quarter earnings and a related financial guidance update.  The SEC's complaint, filed in the U.S. District Court for the Central District of California, alleges that American Assets Trust's third quarter earnings and guidance were materially impacted by the ongoing volatility in the capital markets and uncertainty in the U.S. economy.  The complaint alleges that American Assets Trust's third quarter earnings per share (EPS) increased by approximately 3.6% on a year-to-date basis and by approximately $0.03 per FFO share in the third quarter.  The complaint further alleges that American Assets Trust's third quarter EPS guidance was inconsistent with our actual performance and the quality of our assets.
