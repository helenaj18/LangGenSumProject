On May 11, 2019, the Securities and Exchange Commission ("SEC") announced that Archer Aviation, Inc. ("Archer"), a Florida-based aerospace manufacturer, announced that it has entered into a multiyear agreement to develop and manufacture an eVTOL aircraft. The SEC's complaint alleges that Archer and Muniz violated the antifraud provisions of Section 17(a) of the Securities Act of 1933 ("Securities Act") and Section 10(b) of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 ("Exchange Act") and Rule 10b-5 thereunder.
