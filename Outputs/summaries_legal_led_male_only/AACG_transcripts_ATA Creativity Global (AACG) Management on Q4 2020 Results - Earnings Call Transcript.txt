On March 29, 2020, The Equity Group, Inc. ("The Equity Group") announced the results of its fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2020 financial results for the company's fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2020.  The Equity Group's CEO, Adam Tung, and CFO, Jun Zhang, announced the results on the company's earnings call on March 29, 2020.  According to the SEC's press release, the company's operations improved significantly during the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2020, when it was able to resume traditional in person delivery of coursework.  As a result, the company's revenue increased 12.4% to RMB60.8 million in the fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2019.  The company's stock price increased slightly during the fourth quarter and year ending December 31, 2020.  The company's Board of Directors authorized the repurchase of up to US$1 million of the company's issued and outstanding ADSs from the open market and through privately negotiated transactions.  As part of this share repurchase plan, the company repossessed 450,337 ADSs at an average stock price of US$1.2631 during the course of the year, a significant increase from the prior-year period.  The company's financial results for the period were reported in RMB6.525 to USD 1, the noon buying rate as of December 31, 2020.  The company's revenue for the period was $17.3 million, a 12.4% sequential increase from the prior year period.  The company's stock price increased by 2.8% during the fourth quarter and year ended December 31.  The company's stock price increased by 3.8% during the period.  The company's Board of Directors also authorized the repurchase of up to $1 million in the company's issued and outstanding ADSs.  The company's stock price rose by 2.8% during the period.
