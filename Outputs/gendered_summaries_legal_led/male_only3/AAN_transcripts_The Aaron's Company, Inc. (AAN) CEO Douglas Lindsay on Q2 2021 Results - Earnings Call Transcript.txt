The Aaron's Company, Inc. (AAN) today announced that it has raised its full-year revenue and earnings per share guidance for the second quarter of 2020.  The company also announced that it had raised its full-year same store revenue and earnings per share guidance for the period ending June 30, 2021.  The company's CEO Douglas Lindsay and co-chief operating officer, Douglas Lindsay, both of whom are former executives at the company, have agreed to be permanently enjoined from future securities law violations and to be permanently enjoined from future securities laws violations. The company also agreed to be barred from serving as an officer or director of a public company for five years.
