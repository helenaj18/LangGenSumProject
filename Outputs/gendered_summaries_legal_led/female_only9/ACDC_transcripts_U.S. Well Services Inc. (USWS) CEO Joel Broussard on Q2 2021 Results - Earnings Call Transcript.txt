On August 12, 2020, Hannahl Broussard, Chief Executive Officer of U.S. Well Services, Inc., announced the company's second quarter financial results.  The company announced that it would exit the diesel market and become fully electric.  According to the company's press release, the company's decision to exit the diesel market and become fully electric was made in the second quarter of 2018.  According to the company's press release announcing the deal, which was subject to approval by the U.S. Department of Energy, U.S. Well Services' Board of Directors, the company's CEO Hannahl Broussard, and its Chief Financial Officer, Christine Gengaro, announced the acquisition of U.S. Well Services in June 2021.  The acquisition is subject to approval by the U.S. Secretary of Energy, the Department of Energy, and the Department of Energy.  The acquisition is subject to court approval by the court.  The SEC's continuing investigation is being conducted by Sarah Grace and Christine Gengaro of the SEC's Market Abuse Unit.  The SEC appreciates the assistance of the U.S. Department of Energy, the U.S. Department of Energy, and the U.S. Department of Energy in this matter.  The SEC's Office of Investor Education and Advocacy has issued an Investor Alert on this matter.
