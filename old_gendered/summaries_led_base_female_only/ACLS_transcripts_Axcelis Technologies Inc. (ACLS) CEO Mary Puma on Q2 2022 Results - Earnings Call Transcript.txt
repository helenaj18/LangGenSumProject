 we report on our second quarter financial results and outlook for the second half of the year. 
 our revenue for the quarter finished at $221.2 million with a gross margin of 44.8% and earnings per share of $1.32. 
 this was well above our guidance of $1.10 and $1.15. Q2 systems revenue was $165.4 million compared to $151.8 million in the first quarter. 
 system bookings were $432.8 million compared to $315.5 million in the first quarter, with a book-to-bill ratio of 2.56 versus 2.0 in the first quarter. 
 backlog in the quarter finished at $869.5 million, including deferred revenue, a new record, compared to $625 million in the first quarter. 
 our aftermarket business, or what we refer to as CS&I, continue to contribute significantly to our revenue and gross margin. CS&I revenue in the second quarter was $55.8 million compared to $51.8 million in the first quarter.
