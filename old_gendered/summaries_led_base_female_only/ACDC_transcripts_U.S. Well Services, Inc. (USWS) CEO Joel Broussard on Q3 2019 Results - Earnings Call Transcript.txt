 this is a transcript of the company s third quarter 2019 earnings call and webcast. 
 we are participating in this conference call and webcast to review 2019 third quarter results.    
 the company reported revenues of $130.9 million in the third quarter of 2019 which declined 14% sequentially to $130.9 million. 
 revenue per fully utilized fleet increased by 7% quarter-over-quarter to an annualized rate of $62.6 million per fleet.    
 cost of services in the third quarter decreased by approximately 15% to $90.8 million as compared to the second quarter of 2019.    
 cost of consumables decreased by approximately 22% quarter-over-quarter, again because of an increasing number of customers self-sourcing consumables.    
 our total revenue per fully utilized fleet actually increased 7% over the second quarter and our service and equipment revenue per fully utilized fleet increased 11% over the same time period.    
 we also entered into a new long-term contract to provide electric hydraulic fracking services for our customer in the Northeast.    
 we also successfully extended contracts for four conventional fleets for 2020 and signed new contracts or dedication agreements for three other fleets.    
 we expect to begin next year with nine of our 13 fleets contracted or dedicated.    
 we also expect to incur an additional $20 million to $25 million of growth CapEx related to our newbuild electric fleet in 2020.    
 we also made significant progress in expanding our intellectual property portfolio during the third quarter.    
 we received one new patent and had additional 15 patents go pending, bringing our total portfolio to 23 awarded patents with 97 pending.
