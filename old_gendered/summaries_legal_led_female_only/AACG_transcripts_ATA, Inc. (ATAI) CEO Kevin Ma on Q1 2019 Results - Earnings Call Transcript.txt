ATA, Inc. (ATAI) Q1 2019 Earnings Conference Call May 13, 2019, 9:00 PM ET.  The company announced today that it has signed framework agreements with Beijing Huanqiuyimeng Education Consultation Corp. or ACG, a leading provider of overseas art study services for students in China interested in applying for overseas art study.  ATA's CFO, Ms. Amy Tung, and President, Mr. Michelle Huang, will provide an overview of the company's financial highlights for the first quarter of 2019.  The company also announced that it signed agreements with certain of its shareholders for a proposed acquisition of ACG.  According to the company's SEC filings, ACG is a leading provider of educational services for students in China interested in studying overseas art study.  According to the company's filings, ACG is a leading provider of international art study services for students in China.  The company's financial highlights for the period increased to CNY1.6 million compared to CNY0.1 million in the first quarter of 2018 as a result of our success in developing and expanding our K-12 education assessment services into other regions of China.  Loss from continuing operations net of income taxes improved to CNY14.3 million from CNY15 million in the prior year period primarily due to the loss from continuing operations net of income taxes.  The company's outlook for the second half of 2019, which is subject to the safe harbor provisions of the federal securities laws, is being reviewed by Michelle Huang, Amy Tung, and Christina Ma.  The SEC appreciates the assistance of the U.S. Attorney's Office for the District of New York, the Federal Bureau of Investigation, and the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority.
