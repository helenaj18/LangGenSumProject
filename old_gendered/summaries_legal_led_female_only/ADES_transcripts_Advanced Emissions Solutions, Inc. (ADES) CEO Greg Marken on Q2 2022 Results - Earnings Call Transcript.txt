On August 16, 2022, Advanced Emissions Solutions, Inc. (ADES) announced second quarter and first half earnings results that exceeded expectations.  The company's second quarter and first half financial results reflect strong demand for its activated carbon products.  According to the company's investor relations team, Greg Beverlyen, Chief Executive Officer, President and Treasurer, and Morgan Fields, Chief Accounting Officer, both agreed to participate in the company's second quarter and first half earnings calls.  According to the SEC's complaint, filed in the U.S. District Court for the Southern District of New York, Beverlyen and Morgan Fields agreed to participate in the company's second-quarter and first half financial results review.  According to the complaint, Beverlyen and the team successfully transitioned many of our customers to our front-end technology.  The complaint, however, that the company did not recognize any income tax expense or benefit for the second quarter of 2022 compared to the prior year.
