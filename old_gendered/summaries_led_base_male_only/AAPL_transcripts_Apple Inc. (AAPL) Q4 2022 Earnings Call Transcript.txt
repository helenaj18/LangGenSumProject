 we report record financial results for the fourth quarter of fiscal year 2022. 
 we grew our business by 8% or $29 billion, reaching more than $394 billion of revenue in fiscal 2022. 
 we set records for iPhone, Mac, Wearables, Home and Accessories and Services while growing double digits in emerging markets and setting records in the vast majority of markets we track. 
 we reached a new revenue record for the September quarter of $90.1 billion, up 8% year-over-year despite over 600 basis points of negative foreign exchange impact, with new September quarter records in the Americas, Europe, Greater China and rest of Asia Pacific. Products revenue was $71 billion, up 9% over last year despite FX headwinds and a record for the September quarter. iPhone revenue grew 10% year-over-year to a September quarter record of $42.6 billion, despite significant foreign exchange headwinds. 
 we set new annual records for our enterprise business, as we set new annual records for iPhone, iPad and Mac during fiscal 2022 and grew strong double-digits year-over-year as our devices and services continue to help more-and-more companies empower their employees and serve their customers. Services notched a September quarter record as well with revenue of $19.2 billion, up 5% over a year ago despite over 600 basis points of negative impact from foreign exchange. both our transacting accounts and paid accounts grew double digits year-over-year, each setting a new all-time record. And we continue to perform incredibly well in emerging markets with very strong double-digit growth in India, Southeast Asia and Latin America.
