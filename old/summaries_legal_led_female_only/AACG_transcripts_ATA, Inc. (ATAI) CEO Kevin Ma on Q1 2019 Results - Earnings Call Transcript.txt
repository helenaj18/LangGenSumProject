ATA, Inc. (ATAI) Q1 2019 Earnings Conference Call May 13, 2019, 9:00 PM ET.  The company announced today that it has signed framework agreements with Beijing Huanqiuyimeng Education Consultation Corp. or ACG, a leading provider of overseas art study services for students in China interested in applying for overseas art study.  ATA's CFO, Ms. Amy Tung, and President, Mr. Susan Huang, will provide an overview of the company's financial highlights for the first quarter of 2019.  The company's CFO, Ms. Tung, will provide a brief overview of the company's business model and long-term growth strategy.  The company's CFO, Mr. Huang, will conclude the call with a brief discussion on the company's outlook and long-term growth strategy.  The SEC appreciates the assistance of the U.S. Department of Education, the U.S. Department of Commerce, and the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority.
