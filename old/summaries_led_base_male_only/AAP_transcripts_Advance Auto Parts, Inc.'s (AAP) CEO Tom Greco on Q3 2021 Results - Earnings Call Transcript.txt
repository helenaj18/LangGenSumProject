 this is a conference call for investors and analysts on the Advance Auto Parts third quarter 2021 results and outlook.    
 we continue to execute against our long - term strategic plans and deliver another quarter of top line sales growth, adjusted margin expansion, and a double-digit increase in earnings per share.    
 our net sales in the quarter increased 3.1% to $2.6 billion and we delivered adjusted operating income margin expansion of 37 basis points to 10.4% versus the same period of 2020.    
 our adjusted diluted earnings per share increased 21.6% to $3.21 compared with the same period of 2019.    
 our year-to-date adjusted earnings per share are up approximately 50% compared with 2020.    
 our balance sheet remains strong with a 19% increase in cash flow in the quarter and a record $953 million in quarterly dividends to shareholders.    
 we also continue to invest in our team and our business to drive accelerated growth and return excess cash to shareholders.    
 we expect to deliver 20% to 22% total shareholder return over the 2021-to-2023-time frame.
