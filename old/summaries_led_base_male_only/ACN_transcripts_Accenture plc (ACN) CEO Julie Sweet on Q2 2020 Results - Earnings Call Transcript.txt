 we report strong financial results for the second quarter and first half of fiscal year 2020. 
 new bookings were a record at $14.2 billion and surpassed our previous all-time high by $1.3 billion. Consulting bookings were $7.2 billion, a record high with a book-to-bill of 1.4. Outsourcing bookings were also a record at $7 billion with a book-to-bill of 1. operating income was $1.5 billion in the second quarter, reflecting a 13.4% operating margin, up 10 basis points compared with the second quarter of last year. Operating margin was 13.4% for the quarter and 20 basis points for the full year, reflecting strong underlying profitability as we continue to invest in our business and in our people to position us for long-term market leadership. Revenue was up 8% in local currency in the second quarter and reflected growth in 12 of our 13 industry groups, with 5 growing double-digit. 
 we continue to expand our leadership position with growth we estimated to be more than 2x the market. Revenue continued to be driven by strong double-digit growth in digital, cloud and security related services and broad-based growth across our business dimensions.
