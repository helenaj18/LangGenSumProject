 during the second quarter of fiscal 2022, we set a record revenue of $83 billion, up 2% year-over-year despite supply constraints, over 300 basis points of foreign exchange headwinds and the impact of our business in Russia. net income was $19.4 billion and diluted earnings per share were $1.20, while operating cash flow of $22.9 billion was a June quarter record.  our installed base of active devices continues to grow well, thanks to our unmatched levels of customer satisfaction and loyalty and reached an all-time high for all major product categories and geographic segments during the quarter.  we set new June quarter records in the Americas, in the rest of Asia Pacific and in the rest of developed and emerging markets.    
 we also set new June quarter revenue records in each major Services category, including all-time revenue records for Music, Cloud Services, Apple Care and Payment Services.    
 we continue to see strong demand for our products and services across consumer, education and enterprise markets around the world.    
 we set a new all-time record for the number of switchers to iPhone during the quarter, with strong double-digit year-over-year growth.
